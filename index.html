ABOUT

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to VD Research Chem, a fast growing pharma company in Mohali. Founded in 2024, our mission is to provide high-quality and innovative products to the pharmaceutical industry. We are dedicated to meeting the ever-changing quality needs of our customers and ensuring the safety and efficacy of our products. VD Research Chem, is a research driven company, which specializes in manufactures, sourcing, distribution of pharmaceutical impurity standard, pharmacopeial & non-pharmacopeial reference standards, Drug intermediates, Impurities, drug glucuronides, drug metabolites, Isotope labelled reference standards, specialty chemicals etc. We supply impurity standards and other products as per In house, IP, BP, EP, USP grade with certificate and complete characterization data.

Our strength is our expert and professional team who conduct several challenges in cost cutting and ability to supply quality materials in the short span of time. Currently, we have more than 8,000 Impurity standards in stock with all the characterization data i,e HPLC, Mass, 1H-NMR, 13C-NMR, IR ,TGA, COA,& MSDS, Which can be provided to you in most affordable price with all the supporting data.
